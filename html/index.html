About Us

Empowering students with knowledge, discipline, and confidence.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that shapes responsible, disciplined, and capable students who are prepared for academic and real-world success.

Our Vision

To become a leading educational institution in Ethiopia that inspires innovation, academic excellence, and lifelong learning.

Our History

Established in 1993, our school has grown from a small institution serving the local community into a respected academic center recognized for strong discipline, excellent performance, and modern learning practices. Over the years, we have introduced digital education tools, active student clubs, and improved teaching methodologies.
